01

Set up consultation timings

Video Consultations help to manage the time effectively while allowing a doctor to see more patients. Define suitable consultation timings for video sessions to help manage the time effectively

02

Manage video appointments

Once set up, a doctor can schedule or accept appointments for his telemedicine sessions online. Confirm, reschedule or cancel appointments and patients will be automatically notified of the appointment status.

03

Send consultation summaries

Improve treatment adherence by sharing e-prescriptions,appointment reminders, treatment goals, invoices or even prescribe a monitoring plan for patients, which patients will receive as an email consultation summary after their session.
