Access to medical care is a human right derived from the Universal Declaration of Human Rights(UDHR),proclaimed by United Nations General Assembly in Paris on 10th December,1948.

It is the mission,duty and purpose of E-Meditalk to assist and improve quality of care in remote and underserved areas by using advanced remote technologies to connect healthcare providers and populations for care to sick vunerable populations and charitable purposes.

Our objectives are:

  • Improving patient outcomes
  • Increasing patient engagement and satisfaction
  • Providing remote and rural patients with access to care
  • Improving leverage of limited physician resources
  • Improving specialist efficiency
  • Providing access to new specialties
  • Providing 24/7 access to specialists
